Why Computer Science

I didn't have any experience of coding or cs related in high school. Though I really wish I did, I mainly just stayed active in chemistry related activities, that's probably why I was majoring in chem before. However, I always have a dream or at least goal of coding, to build something myself so I decided to change my major in college. I know it's really really hard, not only courses themselves, but also how the stress would often make me freak out. Me and my friends would often talk about this and it made my stress even worse. In the second semester, I decided to talk about this less wiht my friends and focus more on activities I like. I took part in HackIllinois, which is probably the best decision I made so far. Even though I only did the UI, which is really similar to that one I made for CS125 final project, I really enjoy it. The whole coding environmenet was intimidating at first but I eventually got used to it, and I love it!
